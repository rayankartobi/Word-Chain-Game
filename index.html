Word Chain Game Info

Welcome to the Word Chain Game!

  • Type a word in your chosen language.
  • Each word must start with the last letter of the previous word.
  • vs Computer: Earn 1 point every 5 words. Spend 3 points for a hint!
  • 2 Players (Local): Take turns on the same device!
  • Online Multiplayer: Create a room or join with a code to play with friends!
